About Ejuma

Ejuma is a platform that connects individuals looking for short term jobs to the best freelance job offers. Our main target is senior high school graduates. Usually these graduates just stay with plenty of time to waste after writing their final examinations. Instead of just staying home doing nothing or engaging in frivolous activities,they can gain employment in institutions to work for a short period of time. Finding companies that hire freelancers can be a very tedious task.

This is where Ejuma comes in! We sign up top industries that employ freelancers and people looking for freelance jobs. Job seekers recieve notifications of top jobs available and employers are notified when freelancers seek short term employment in their institutions.

Looking for the latest jobs in town?

Ejuma bring you the best job offers. We are just a click away!
